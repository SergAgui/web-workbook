Welcome to my blog page!

Follow my weekly blog where we explore all the different types of keys throughout history. Whether they are physical keys that require a specific lock or a look into the world of pop culture and how they have become a fashion statement!

Multi Lock Keys!!

Did you know that multifuncional keys create a complete key which you can use with a transponder/lock of choice?

Old Keys!!

Did you know that Linus Yale, Jr. was inspired by the 1840s pin-tumbler lock that was originally designed by his father?

Fancy Keys!!

Did you know that some celebrities, such as Pharrell or Tyler the Creator, enjoy wearing keys as a fashion statement?

Teething Keys!!

Did you know that teething keys provide exercise for a baby's gums and teeth as well as providing visual stimulation?

Alicia Keys!!

Did you know that Alicia Keys, the American singer, songwriter, record producer, pianist, and activist, was born as Alicia Augello Cook?

Drum Keys!!

Did you know that drum keys are a tool used to adjust the tension rods of a Drum?

Piano Keys!!

Did you know that when you press down on a piano key, a small hammer shoots up and strikes the corresponding strings from beneath?

Keyboard Keys!!

Did you know that the Key Tronic Corporation was one of the first in keyboard tech to not use self-contained switches?
